About Rezzy Rate

Rezzy is a smart resume checker designed to help job seekers instantly evaluate their resumes against industry expectations.

Our tool analyzes your text for:

  • ATS keyword coverage – how well your resume matches job descriptions.
  • Professional writing quality – readability, active voice, and clarity.
  • Structure – ensuring key sections like experience, education, and skills are present.
  • Readability – simple, scannable, recruiter-friendly language.

Rezzy doesn’t just give you a score — it also provides actionable suggestions to improve your resume and stand out.
