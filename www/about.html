About Rezzy Rate

Rezzy Rate is a fast, friendly resume checker that helps you speak the language of recruiters and Applicant Tracking Systems – without sounding like a robot.

AI-powered resume feedback Built for real-world job searches Actionable tips in seconds

Why Rezzy Rate exists

Job hunting is already stressful. Between tailoring your resume, decoding job descriptions, and wondering if a human will ever actually read your application, it’s easy to feel stuck.

Rezzy Rate was created to make that process less confusing and a lot more predictable. Instead of guessing what matters, you get clear, prioritized feedback on the things recruiters and ATS actually pay attention to.

Think of Rezzy Rate as a brutally honest (but kind) hiring manager that sits on your shoulder and says: “Here’s what’s strong, here’s what’s weak, and here’s exactly what to fix next.”

What Rezzy Rate looks at

  • ATS keyword coverage – how well your resume lines up with the job description.
  • Writing quality – clarity, tone, readability, and fluff that can be cut.
  • Structure & sections – Experience, Education, Skills, and how cleanly they’re organized.
  • Scanability – bullet structure, use of metrics, and how quickly a recruiter can skim.
  • Impact statements – whether your bullets sound like “I did stuff” or “I moved the needle.”

You don’t just get a score – you get a roadmap of what to tweak so your next application feels intentional, not random.

How Rezzy Rate works

  1. Upload or paste your resume
    Drop in your PDF, DOCX, or text, or paste your resume directly into the checker.
  2. Optionally add a job description
    This lets Rezzy Rate analyze keyword alignment and highlight important gaps for the specific role.
  3. Get an instant breakdown
    In a few seconds, you’ll see scores, strengths, and specific suggestions you can apply today.

No templates to wrestle with. No fluff. Just practical feedback you can use between applying to jobs, during a lunch break, or right before hitting “submit.”

Who Rezzy Rate is for

  • Career starters – students, recent grads, and career changers who want a resume that doesn’t feel generic.
  • Mid-career pros – people who have solid experience but need help translating it into sharp bullet points.
  • Job search grinders – anyone firing off lots of applications and wanting a quick sanity check before each one.

What makes it different

  • Plain-language feedback – no dense reports, just clear notes you can act on right away.
  • Built around real recruiter habits – highlights what’s skimmed first, ignored, or overused.
  • Fast and lightweight – no account creation needed to try it, and you can test small changes quickly.

The goal isn’t to “game” the system. It’s to help you present your experience in a way that feels sharp, honest, and easy to say “yes” to.

FAQ

Do I have to be a “tech person” to use Rezzy Rate?

Not at all. If you can upload a file or paste text, you can use Rezzy Rate. The feedback is written in normal language, not technical jargon.

Does Rezzy Rate write my resume for me?

Rezzy Rate doesn’t replace your judgment or your voice. It highlights what’s strong, what’s unclear, and where you can add detail – so you can rewrite your resume in a way that still sounds like you.

Can I use it for multiple job applications?

Yes. Rezzy Rate works best when you tweak your resume for each role. You can run different versions against different job descriptions to see how your alignment improves over time.

Is Rezzy Rate a guarantee I’ll get interviews?

No tool can guarantee that. What Rezzy Rate does do is increase your odds by helping you present a clearer, sharper, and more targeted version of your experience.
