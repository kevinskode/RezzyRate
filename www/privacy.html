Privacy Policy

Simple promise: We don’t sell your data. We keep things minimal and transparent.

What we collect

  • Resume text you paste — used only to analyze and produce your results.
  • Basic usage data — anonymous metrics to improve reliability and features.

What we don’t collect

  • No account profile data unless you explicitly create one.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No sale of personal information. Ever.

How your data is handled

  • Your pasted text is processed to generate a report and improvements.
  • Temporary processing may occur in memory; we avoid storing raw resumes whenever possible.
  • Any optional saved results are encrypted at rest.

Your choices

  • You can request deletion of any saved results at any time.
  • You can use Rezzy without creating an account.
